ABOUT ME

My name is Michal Ruprecht. I am a metro Detroit-based amateur potter currently finishing my last year at the University of Michigan. I began my practice in 2022 as a student of Professor Susan Crowell and aim to create pieces that examine the intricacies of nature and the human body.

I draw inspiration from the rich history of Michiganâ€™s Great Lakes, human anatomy, and yoga poses. I am fascinated by the binding problem of neurobiology: how our brain synthesizes conscious awareness through the integration of various stimuli. I am also interested in using art to communicate science and health issues.

I practice sculpting and coil and slab building and I am learning to build on the wheel. I can be reached at me@michalruprecht.com.
