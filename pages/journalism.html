ABOUT ME.

I am a Detroit-based journalist currently finishing my last year at the University of Michigan. I aim to write disruptive stories that lead to improved scientific and medical literacy in medically underserved communities. I’ve been a journalist since 2014 and have reported on a wide range of beats including research, environment and healthcare. My research in neuroscience, clinical microbiology, biochemistry and chemistry labs has laid the foundation of my passion for science. While my journey to medicine was influenced by my upbringing in Far Rockaway, NY and work with residents affected by the Flint water crisis.

This website is a manifest of what I have learned and how I’ve evolved. I can be reached at me@michalruprecht.com.
