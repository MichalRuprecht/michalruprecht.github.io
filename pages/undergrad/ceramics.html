ABOUT ME

My name is Michal Ruprecht. I am a ceramics student currently finishing my last year at the University of Michigan. I began my practice in 2022 and I aim to create pieces that examine the intricacies of nature.

I draw inspiration from the rich history of Michiganâ€™s Great Lakes, human anatomy, trees, mushrooms, and yoga poses. I am fascinated by the binding problem of neurobiology: how our brain synthesizes conscious awareness through the integration of various stimuli. I am also interested in using art to communicate science and health issues.

I throw on the wheel, practice sculpting, and coil and slab build. I can be reached at me@michalruprecht.com.
